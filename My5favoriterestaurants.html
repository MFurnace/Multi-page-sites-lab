My 5 Favorite Restaurants

Two of the best places I have ever eaten were the loving confines of my Grandmother's kitchen. Granma Angie specialized in the cuisine of her hometown of New Orleans. The savory and richness of her gumbo, stuffed bell peppers, red beans and rice are flavors I will never forget. Equally as delicious was the southern stylings of my Grandma Ozie. I have vivid memories of our Saturday breakfast. It seemed like everything she touch was delicious. Perhaps I am most fond of her caramel cake- to this day, I have not met a "caramel cake" that comes close to it. But then again, there was the lemon jello cake.

With that said, here are are a few places that serve pretty tasty food. If you are ever in the cities below...check 'em out!
